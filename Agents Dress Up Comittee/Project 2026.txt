### Weekly Rhythm & Rituals
- **Core Principles:**
    -   **Morning Power Block:** The three deep work sessions are prioritized for the morning.
    -   **Meetings Count:** Any significant meeting will count as one of your deep work blocks.
    -   **Afternoons for Connection:** Afternoons are then free for collaborative projects with friends, personal growth, and dating.

- **The Days & Their Focus:**
    -   **Mystical Monday (Moon ☽):** Reflection, planning, and core work. The perfect day to release foundational updates for **Chronos Engine**.
        -   Work on **Metaplasma & SCRY**.
        -   **Exercise:** Yoga.
        -   **Wellness:** Extended meditation and breathwork sessions.
        -   **Core Chore:** Laundry & Watering Plants.
        -   **Freetime:** 1 hour buffer for spontaneity or rest.
    -   **Tactical Tuesday (Mars ♂):** Action and focused aggression. The designated day for making progress on **Shinobi Tactics**.
        -   Work on **Chronos Engine**.
        -   **Exercise:** Fighter-style training and martial arts.
        -   **Social:** Board Game Night (Chess or Shinobi Tactics), Chess Study, Puzzles.
        -   **Ritual:** Warrior Meal, gear-related checks.
        -   **Core Chore:** Taking out Trash & Recycling.
        -   **Freetime:** 1 hour buffer for spontaneity or rest.
    -   **Wired Wednesday (Mercury ☿):** Communication and connection. Reserved for **meetings, sales, networking, and outreach**.
        -   Release a new beat/song.
        -   Post short-form videos and tweets.
        -   Update websites and perform fan/client outreach.
        -   Check and maintain gadgets, tech, and digital tools.
        -   **Core Chore:** Paying Bills, Sorting Mail, & Running Errands.
        -   **Freetime:** 1 hour buffer for spontaneity or rest.
    -   **Thrive Thursday (Jupiter ♃):** Growth and expansion. The official **"Coffee Date Day"** for meeting new people.
        -   Focus on promotion, advertising, and marketing funnels.
        -   Create content, manage scheduled posts, and send newsletters.
        -   **Self-Care:** Do something luxurious.
        -   **Core Chore:** Decluttering & Donations.
        -   **Freetime:** 1 hour buffer for spontaneity or rest.
    -   **Flourish Friday (Venus ♀):** Art, beauty, and relationships. The day for releasing creative **ADUC updates** and for the recurring evening **"Date Night"**.
        -   Release a new beat/song.
        -   Create visual content and assets.
        -   Engage in music: play, sing, or rap.
        -   **Activities:** Shopping, style/wardrobe planning, photoshoots/music videos.
        -   **Food:** Enjoy fun food.
        -   **Core Chore:** Changing bed linens & tidying for the evening.
        -   **Freetime:** 1 hour buffer for spontaneity or rest.
    -   **Structure Saturday (Saturn ♄):** Discipline and systems. For reviewing progress, organizing, and maintaining the structures that support your goals.
        -   **Tasks:** File/digital clean-ups, weekly/monthly/quarterly/yearly reviews, grocery shopping, finances, backups.
        -   **Activities:** Handy work/carpentry, hangouts.
        -   **Wellness:** Go for a long walk, rest, and reflect.
        -   **Core Chore:** Groceries, Deep Cleaning, & Maintenance.
        -   **Freetime:** 1 hour buffer for spontaneity or rest.
    -   **Sacred Sunday (Sun ☉):** Rest and personal expression. For self-care, hobbies, and connecting with your core purpose.
        -   **Mind:** Dream decoding, lucid dreaming practice, ask 1 deep question.
        -   **Creativity:** Freestyle creative block, art/dream art, write a poem.
        -   **Study:** Long reading block, Deep Work Study Block #1 (Japanese), Deep Work Study Block #2 (Flex).
        -   **Wellness:** Nature walk, long meditation, extended evening routine, "Sacred Soup."
        -   **Zen:** Tree climbing, stargazing, sun tanning, herb gathering.
        -   **Social:** Dates.
        -   **Core Chore:** Simple evening tidy-up.
        -   **Freetime:** 1 hour buffer for spontaneity or rest.
