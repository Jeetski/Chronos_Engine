Paid Feature Ideas for Chronos

1. Money & Business Pack
- Commands, modules, widgets, panels, and wizards for managing budgets, tracking revenue/expenses, scheduling grant deadlines, and aligning financial goals with daily commitments.
- Views that synthesize cash-flow trends, invoices, targets, and investment notes; automated reminders for billing cycles.
- Integration helpers for connecting to external financial data or importing ledger-style YAML entries.

2. Health & Fitness Pack
- Modules and widgets for logging water intake, meals, workouts, supplements, and recovery metrics.
- Routines wizard that schedules exercise sessions, rest days, and progressive overload adjustments with habit linkages.
- Dashboards that combine biometrics, mood, and workout summaries while allowing quick habit edits.

3. Mystical Pack
- Astrology integrations with planetary hours, transits, numerology overlays, and themed days/weeks (e.g., “Year of the Cat,” “Mercury Retrograde Ritual”).
- Magical association themes for schedule slots, tarot spreads, rune draws, I Ching readings, and other divinations with Chronos items.
- Widgets that surface suggested rituals, chants, or focus prompts based on current cosmic alignments.

4. Mindful Pack
- Yoga, meditation, and spiritual practice modules: pose sequences, breathing timers, mindful journaling prompts.
- Zen modes that declutter UI, enforce focus blocks, and present minimalist themes tied to the user’s intention.
- Specialized journals or sacred spaces for gratitude, compassion, and presence work, complete with reminders or ritual cues.

5. Sleep & Dreams Pack
- Expanded dream journal that supports multi-entry nights, tags, and quick voice-to-text capture for hazy recollections.
- Dream analysis engine that applies code-like syntax highlighting to symbols, archetypes, recurring motifs, and emotional tones, storing them as structured tokens.
- Symbol tracker that charts frequency, combinations, and evolution of motifs over time with comparative visuals (personal vs. collective archetypes).
- Dictionary layer that merges multiple dream dictionaries and mythic references, surfacing relevant interpretations inline and linking to deeper study cards.
- Sleep tracking dashboards that combine wearable/imported data, Chronos routines, and recovery metrics to correlate dream quality with bedtime habits.
- Smart prompts for pre-sleep intention setting, lucid-dream cues, and morning reflection workflows that feed directly into the journal and analytics.

6. Entrepreneur Pack
- Solo founder cockpit with pitch-ready task pipelines, funding trackers, and investor update templates aligned to sprint cycles.
- Market and customer intelligence dashboards that surface surveys, illustrations, and traction metrics next to the product roadmap.
- Revenue and runway widgets that accept CRM, ledger, and payment data while highlighting deal stages, taxes, and compliance reminders.
- Coaching prompts and accountability rituals for experiments, launches, and founder wellness, all tied to Chronos routines and focus rooms.
- Collaboration helpers for contractors, advisors, and communities: shared belief boards, action approvals, and timeline syncing across ecosystems.
- Content orchestration tools (calendars, campaign briefs, editorial reminders) that stay in sync with launch plans and social signals.
