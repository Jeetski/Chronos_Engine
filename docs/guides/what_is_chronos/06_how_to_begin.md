# How To Begin

Do not try to model your entire life at once.

Start small.

A good way to begin is:

1. Pick one part of your day.
2. Turn it into one routine.
3. Break that routine into smaller parts.
4. Add a few concrete items.
5. Let the structure grow naturally over time.

For example:
- start with a morning routine
- split it into hygiene, movement, and planning
- break one of those into smaller repeatable steps

That is enough.

Chronos becomes powerful gradually. You do not need a perfect system on day one. You need a simple structure you can actually use.

Once that first piece works, you can expand into:
- more routines
- fuller days
- stronger weeks
- bigger goals and systems

The goal is not to build everything immediately.

The goal is to create a life structure that you can return to.
