# What Is Chronos?

This guide is a short introduction to Chronos for brand new users.

It explains Chronos at a high level:
- what it is
- why it exists
- how it structures life
- what that structure looks like in practice
- the philosophy behind it
- how to begin without overwhelm

Guide steps:
1. `01_what_chronos_is.md`
2. `02_why_chronos_exists.md`
3. `03_hierarchy.md`
4. `04_structure_in_practice.md`
5. `05_why_it_matters.md`
6. `06_how_to_begin.md`

If Chronos feels unusual at first, that is normal. It is not just a to-do app or a calendar. It is a way of giving visible structure to your life so your days, routines, and goals can support each other.
